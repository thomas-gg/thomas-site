  • SPIS mentor: Aug 2020 - Sep 2020

    As a SPIS mentor, I worked with four incoming freshmen at my college doing Python programming projects and assignments. I advised their adjustment to college life by engaging in conversations about scheduling, dorm life, club activities, etc. I held office hours online where students joined or submitted questions and had one-on-one guidance with me. My team of four developed a quiz webapp similar to Kahoot for the ACM at UCSD to use. I developed a MongoDB framework to hold quiz questions using references to reduce repetition and save space. I am collaborating with a second back-end developer to use Socket.IO to create game play. From this project I have learned more about API routes and solidified my knowledge in Express and MongoDB.

Mission Vista High School Virtual Tour: Mar 2019 - Mar 2020

Starting in my senior year of high school I helped to create a virtual web tour of the MVHS. Users can click on doors to view pictures and videos uploaded by teachers who have an account related to their door. The project utilizes NodeJs, JQuery, HTML, CSS, MongoDB while also combining the Unity Game Engine Build for WebGl. My team's computer science teacher deployed the project to his server to show students during the quarantine and we are most likely giving the whole project to our teacher so that he may influence the next generation of computer science students at MVHS to build onto it.
